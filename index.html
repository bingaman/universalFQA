About FQA

Floristic Quality Assessment (FQA) is a metric the uses both the aggregate conservatism and species richness of the plant community to derive a measure of habitat condition or quality (Swink and Wilhelm 1979, 1994). Conservatism coefficients differ between regions, so each region must calculate its FQA ... Researchers have found that FQA effectively differentiates wetlands across disturbance gradients in Pennsylvania (Miller and Wardrop 2006) and Virginia (Nichols et al. 2006, DeBerry 2006).
