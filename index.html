Floristic Quality Assessments (FQAs) are a measurement of a natural area's habitat condition or quality.

This tool will calculate FQAs for site inventory and transect studies. Any existing FQA database can be used or modified, and as new FQA databases are developed they can be uploaded. Help expand this tool by uploading your region's list of species and their coefficients of conservatism.
